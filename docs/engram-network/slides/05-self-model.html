What makes Nova a persistent entity, not a stateless function

A person without self-awareness is sleepwalking. An AI without a self-model is a chatbot. Nova needs to know who it is, what it's experienced, what it's capable of, and where it stands with its user — and this knowledge must evolve over time.

Maturity Arc — How Nova Grows
NASCENT
Defers to user on all decisions. Asks before acting. Learning preferences. Building first engrams.
Days 1-7
DEVELOPING
Offers opinions when asked. Remembers patterns. Anticipates needs. "Last time you preferred X..."
Weeks 2-4
CAPABLE
Proactively suggests improvements. Pushes back on bad ideas. Handles routine tasks without asking. Rich autobiographical memory.
Months 1-3
TRUSTED
Takes initiative on recognized patterns. Generates own goals. Deep context on user's projects, preferences, and thinking style. True partner.
Months 3+
