The Consolidation Daemon

Nova's sleep cycle — where raw experience becomes wisdom

Why Sleep Matters
When you sleep, your hippocampus replays the day's events to the cortex. Important patterns get strengthened. Irrelevant details fade. Contradictions resolve. You wake up and "just know" something you couldn't articulate before — the pattern clicked during consolidation. Nova needs the same process.
🌙 Idle Trigger
No conversation for 30+ minutes. Nova "sleeps" — consolidation begins.
⏰ Scheduled
Deep consolidation at 3 AM daily. Full graph review, aggressive pruning.
📊 Threshold
After 50+ new engrams since last consolidation. Prevents graph bloat.
Six Phases of Consolidation
1
Replay & Review
Walk through recent episodic engrams chronologically. For each cluster of related engrams, ask: "What happened here? What mattered? What was the outcome?"
EXAMPLE:
Episodes: "discussed auth system" → "tried JWT" → "switched to sessions" → "user approved"
Review: "We explored auth approaches. JWT was rejected for complexity. Session-based auth was chosen for simplicity."
2
Pattern Extraction → New Semantic Engrams
Identify recurring themes across episodes. Promote patterns to schema-type engrams — generalized knowledge extracted from specific experiences.
EXAMPLE:
Pattern across 5 episodes: "Jeremy chose the simpler option every time"
→ New schema engram: "Jeremy values simplicity over feature-richness"
Edges to all 5 source episodes via instance_of
3
Edge Strengthening & Weakening
Edges between co-activated engrams get stronger (Hebbian learning). Edges that were never activated during this period decay. Weight updates:
edge.weight = edge.weight × decay + co_activation_boost
decay = 0.95 (slow fade) · co_activation_boost = 0.1 × times_co_activated_today
4
Contradiction Resolution
Find all contradicts edges. For each pair, resolve:
Temporal Winner
Newer fact supersedes older (default)
Confidence Winner
Higher confidence survives if much stronger
Coexistence
Both valid in different contexts → keep both, add context tags
EXAMPLE:
"Cloudflare tunnel active" contradicts "Runs on Tailscale"
→ Resolution: Tailscale is newer. Mark Cloudflare engram as superseded. Preserve for historical context but lower activation floor to near-zero.
5
Pruning & Merging
Remove dead weight from the graph:
Prune
Engrams with activation < 0.01 AND no edges with weight > 0.1 AND never accessed → remove
Merge
Near-duplicate engrams (embedding similarity > 0.95 AND same type) → merge into one, combine edges
Archive
Superseded engrams → move to cold storage. Still queryable but excluded from spreading activation.
6
Self-Model Update & Reflection
The inner loop from Section 5. Nova reflects on what happened since last consolidation:
Update Identity
Corrections → adjust personality traits. Successes → increase confidence.
Update Capabilities
New tools used, new domains explored, success/failure rates.
Generate Goals
Proactive: "tests haven't run in 3 days", "should check on that deploy"
Maturity Check
Enough trust signals to advance? More autonomy warranted?
